Supported Chart Types

Visualizing your database results.

ARIA automatically selects the best chart type for your data, but you can always explicitly ask for a specific visualization in your question.

Available Visualizations

  • 📊 Bar Chart: Best for comparing discrete categories (e.g., "Revenue by region").
  • 📈 Line Chart: Ideal for showing trends over time (e.g., "Monthly signups over the last year").
  • 📉 Area Chart: Similar to a line chart but highlights the magnitude of change over time.
  • 🥧 Pie Chart: Perfect for showing proportions of a whole (e.g., "Market share by product").
  • 🔵 Scatter Plot: Best for observing relationships between two numerical variables.
  • 📋 Data Grid (Table): When exact numbers matter, ARIA presents a clean, paginated data table.
💡 Changing Charts on the Fly

If ARIA generates a Bar chart but you prefer a Line chart, just type: "Change this to a line chart". ARIA will instantly re-render the view without re-querying the database!
