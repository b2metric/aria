Team Memory (Mem0)

How ARIA learns and retains your organization's unique business logic.

ARIA isn't just a stateless bot. It uses a vector database (Qdrant) combined with the Mem0 framework to build a long-term memory of how your business operates.

Types of Memory

ARIA categorizes knowledge into three distinct scopes:

  1. User Preferences: "I always want to see revenue grouped by month." ARIA learns your individual habits. (Decays after 6 months to stay fresh).
  2. Team Conventions: "In the Data Science team, active users are those who logged in within 30 days." Shared across all members of a department.
  3. Query Cache: Successful Natural Language to SQL mappings are saved. When someone asks a similar question, ARIA recalls the exact SQL without guessing.
💡 Managing Team Conventions

Administrators and Team Leads can view, edit, or manually inject specific rules into ARIA's brain by navigating to Admin > Team Conventions in the UI.

How Memory Influences AI

Before ARIA generates any SQL, it performs a semantic search against the Memory database using your question. The top rules and past query examples are silently injected into the LLM's prompt, effectively grounding the AI in your company's specific reality.
