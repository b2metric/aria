Why ARIA?

How ARIA compares to other AI and BI tools on the market.

If you're evaluating ARIA for your organization, you might wonder how it stands out against general AI chatbots (like ChatGPT) or enterprise ecosystem tools (like Microsoft Fabric Copilot).

ARIA vs. Ecosystem Copilots (e.g., MS Fabric)

Ecosystem tools often require a massive upfront investment in both time and infrastructure.

  • No Data Migration Required: To use tools like Microsoft Fabric Copilot, you must move your data into their cloud (OneLake) and build heavy semantic models. ARIA connects directly to your existing databases (Oracle, PostgreSQL) right where they live.
  • On-Premise Ready: ARIA can be deployed entirely on-premise, making it the perfect choice for highly regulated industries like Banking and Telco.
  • Learn by Chatting: Instead of coding DAX measures, ARIA's Team Memory learns your business rules just by chatting with you.

ARIA vs. General AI (ChatGPT / Claude)

Standard AI models are great at writing code, but they are not enterprise BI tools.

  • Privacy First: Your database schema stays within your secure tenant. General AIs often use inputs for training data.
  • End-to-End Execution: ChatGPT gives you a SQL query that you must copy-paste into another tool. ARIA generates the SQL, executes it securely, handles errors, and draws a beautiful interactive chart—all in 5 seconds.
  • Database Safeguards: ARIA actively protects your database with SELECT-only guards and EXPLAIN dry-runs, preventing massive queries from crashing your production systems.
💡 The ARIA Advantage

ARIA bridges the gap between the speed of conversational AI and the rigorous security and governance required by enterprise IT departments.
