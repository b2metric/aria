Data Exports (MinIO)

How ARIA handles artifact generation, image downloads, and CSV exports.

Whenever you generate a report or a chart, ARIA doesn't just display it on the screen—it securely preserves a copy of the raw data and visual artifact.

S3-Compatible Object Storage

ARIA utilizes MinIO (an S3-compatible highly scalable object storage service) to manage artifacts behind the scenes.

1. CSV Exports

Every successful SQL execution that returns data generates a temporary CSV file. A secure, pre-signed download link (valid for 24-72 hours depending on your configuration) is attached to your chat message. You can download the exact raw data behind any chart simply by clicking "Download CSV".

2. Large Data Handling (Background Workers)

If you request an enormous dataset (e.g. 50,000 rows) that exceeds the browser's rendering capacity but stays within your Tenant Row Limit, ARIA intercepts the request.

Instead of freezing your screen, ARIA delegates the execution to a Background Worker. The worker fetches the data asynchronously, writes it to MinIO, and delivers a download link directly into your chat.

⚠️ Retention Policy

Data exports and CSV artifacts are considered ephemeral. They are automatically cleared from the MinIO bucket based on your organization's artifact retention policy to ensure data privacy and minimize storage costs.
