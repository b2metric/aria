# Why ARIA?

Classic BI dashboards answer **pre-defined** questions; anything new means a new development
request. ARIA adds a **natural-language layer**: ask an open-ended question, get an answer in
seconds — while your data stays in your own warehouse and access stays governed.

- **Self-service:** business users ask their own questions; the data team keeps governance.
- **On your data, your rules:** queries run in your warehouse; role-based SQL visibility; row & token limits.
- **Not just a number:** every answer comes with a chart and a business insight.
